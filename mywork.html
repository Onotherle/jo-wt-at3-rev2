grand slammers.

Tennis Club Web Design.

Although crude, one of my earliest online projects and works in web development was a website design for Grand Slammers Tennis Club.

This is one of the projects which developed my HTML and CSS knowledge, and the foundation of my technical web design skills.

For the Home page, one of the specifications was to include a hero image with the club slogan:

"Slamming our way to health and happiness"

The About Us page needed the organisation's mission statement, members and club history with a link to their affiliate, Tennis West.

Links to the organisation's socials needed to be included in the simple footer layout. This includes a general copyright notice.

A functioning form is included in the Membership page, which sends the entered information as an email once the form is submitted.

Additionally, there are links provided to organisational policies and procedures. These open in a new tab when clicked.

Finally, in addition to basic contact information, the Contact Us page needed an integrated Google Maps display to the local tennis court.

Developing this simple website has considerably improved my fundamental knowledge of the applications of HTML and CSS for web development.
